Executive Summary

  1. 1 AI accelerates discovery but introduces novel risks in citation accuracy and provenance.
  2. 2 RAG and structured retrieval reduce hallucinations when sources are governed and auditable.
  3. 3 Attribution standards are emerging; early adoption lowers legal and reputational exposure.
  4. 4 Watermarking and signed citations enable verifiable chains of reference.
  5. 5 Organizations need policy, training, and tooling to ensure traceable, compliant referencing.
